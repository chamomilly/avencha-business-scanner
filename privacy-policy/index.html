Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Avencha ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Avencha mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account in Avencha, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials
  • User profile information

This information is used to create and manage your account and allow you to access features within the app.

Location Data

Avencha uses precise location data to provide map-based gameplay and navigation features.

Location information may be used to:

  • Show map locations
  • Guide users through map steps
  • Provide location-based experiences within the app

Location data is only used while the app is actively in use unless the user grants additional permissions.

Users can disable location permissions at any time through their device settings.

Usage Data and Analytics

We may automatically collect certain information about how the app is used, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App interactions
  • Crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics

This information helps us improve the reliability and functionality of the app.

Purchases

Avencha may offer in-app purchases. Payments are processed securely through the platform provider:

  • Purchases on Android are processed through Google Play
  • Purchases on iOS are processed through Apple

We do not collect or store payment card information. Payment processing is handled entirely by the platform provider.

Third-Party Services

Avencha uses trusted third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

These services may include:

  • Firebase (authentication, analytics, backend services)
  • Google Maps (map services and location features)
  • Platform services provided by Google and Apple

These services may collect information such as device identifiers, usage data, and diagnostic information.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Enable location-based gameplay
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Process purchases
  • Improve features and performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information.

However, no system of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data.

Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion at any time by contacting us using the information below.

Upon receiving a valid request, we will delete or anonymize personal data associated with the account unless retention is required by law.

Children's Privacy

Avencha is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the ability to:

  • Request access to personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information

Requests can be made using the contact details below.
